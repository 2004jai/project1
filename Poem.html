Dance Of Love

The day we danced, it was so sweet,
Our bodies swayed to the love we'd meet,
You were so close, I felt your heat,
And time stopped still, under our feet.

Your hands on my shoulder, mine on your waist,
My heart was pounding, a steady pace,
As we moved to the music's grace,
I glimpsed an angel, in your face.

Lost in the moment, we danced away,
In an open field, where flowers sway,
The wind blew gently, with hair astray,
And I was lost in your eyes that day.

I didn't care about the teacher's rules,
Or the other students, who played the fools,
I only cared about the love in our grooves,
And how I felt, in your beautiful view.

But now that love is long gone,
And all that's left, is a scarred song,
The memory of that dance lingers on,
And I'll cherish it everyday till I'm gone .......

Absence

In love's absence, pain runs deep,
Unseen wounds, secrets we keep,
Yet even if we part, my heart holds true,
Feelings endure, forever for you.


Gazing at the moon, a tender guide,
Reminding me of lessons inside,
Sent by God, you were meant to be,
A chapter of love's destiny.


I loved you then, and I love you still,
A flame unyielding, against all will,
Through distance and time, our bond remains,
In eternal love's sacred domains.
